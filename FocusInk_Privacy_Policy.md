# FocusInk Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 17 July 2026**

FocusInk is a browser extension that provides cursor-following word and paragraph highlighting to support focused reading on webpages.

## Information handled

To provide its highlighting feature, FocusInk processes the following information locally on the user's device:

- **Website content:** text displayed on the webpage, only as needed to identify the word or readable text block under the user's pointer.
- **User activity:** the current pointer position and pointer movement, only as needed to determine what should be highlighted.
- **Extension preferences:** enabled or disabled state, focus mode, intensity, highlight color, and saturation.

FocusInk does not transmit webpage content, pointer information, visited URLs, browsing history, or extension preferences to the developer or to any third party.

## How information is used

The information above is used only to provide FocusInk's visible cursor-following highlighting feature and to remember the user's chosen appearance settings.

## Local storage

FocusInk stores extension preferences locally through `chrome.storage.local`. These preferences remain on the user's device until the user changes them, clears the extension's stored data, or uninstalls the extension.

## Data sharing and sale

FocusInk does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

## External services and remote code

FocusInk does not use analytics, advertising services, external servers, or remotely hosted JavaScript or WebAssembly.

## Data security

Because FocusInk does not transmit user data to external servers, webpage content and pointer information remain on the user's device during normal use.

## Changes to this policy

If FocusInk's data practices change, this privacy policy will be updated before the changed version is published.
